Inspired Ventures was founded on a simple premise: growth happens outside the comfort zone.

After becoming the first NFL player to climb the Seven Summits, Mark Pattison realized that the lessons learned at 29,000 feet were directly applicable to leadership, business, and life. The "Seven Summits Summit" is the physical manifestation of that journey.
