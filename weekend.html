ESSENTIAL GEAR

  • 01. BROKEN-IN BOOTS Do not bring new boots. Your feet are your engine. Protect them.
  • 02. HARD SHELL LAYERS Weather turns fast at 9,000ft. Waterproof shell + puffer. No cotton.
  • 03. HYDRATION (2L+) CamelBak or Nalgenes. High altitude dehydrates you instantly.
  • 04. ANALOG TOOLS Notebook and pen. Digital noise is the enemy of clarity.

FIELD NOTES (FAQ)

HOW FIT DO I NEED TO BE?

Comfortable walking 3-5 miles at altitude. If you can walk 18 holes of golf, you can do this.

WILL I HAVE CELL SERVICE?

Yes, but we encourage "Airplane Mode". Look up, not down.

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

"Mountain Ready." Function over fashion. Leave the blazer at home.
