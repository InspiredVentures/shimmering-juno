04. HYDRATION: 2 Liters minimum capacity. Water bladder (CamelBak) or Nalgene bottles.
05. SUN PROTECTION: High altitude sun is unforgiving. Sunglasses, hat, and sunscreen are mandatory.

BASE CAMP & EVENING

01. MOUNTAIN CASUAL: Technical pants, flannels, comfortable shoes. We prioritize warmth and mobility over style.
02. ANALOG TOOLS: A dedicated notebook and pen. You will be writing a lot.
03. RECOVERY: Any personal supplements or recovery tools (foam roller, etc) you swear by.

Field Notes (FAQ)

How fit do I need to be?

You should be comfortable walking 3-5 miles on uneven terrain. We have pace groups for all levels, but this is an active weekend. If you can walk an 18-hole golf course, you can do this.

Is altitude a factor?

Yes. Sun Valley is at 5,900ft. Hydration starts on the plane ride here. Drink plenty of water before you arrive.

What is the dress code?

"Mountain Ready". Hiking pants, technical layers, and functional footwear. We want you comfortable and ready to move. We recommend leaving the denim at home.

Will I have cell service?

Yes, but we strongly encourage you to disconnect. Use this time to look up, not down.

What if it rains?

We hike. Unless there is lightning or severe danger, the schedule holds. Bring a waterproof shell.

Dietary Restrictions?

We cater to all standard requirements (GF, DF, V). You will be able to specify this during registration.
