THE WEEKEND

MORE THAN A CONFERENCE. A CALL TO ACTION.

D1

DAY ONE: CONTEXT & FOUNDATION

FRIDAY: 17:00 - 22:00

You’ll hear from a small group of keynote speakers who’ve built real things and navigated real transitions. Honest stories about what worked, what didn’t, and what they learned the hard way.

Then we set the weekend up properly: how the SUMMITS methodology works, how your trail groups will run, and how to use these three days to get clear on what matters next.

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • A clear understanding of the SUMMITS roadmap and how we’ll apply it across the weekend
  • • Your first draft of an Everest worth pursuing now, in business, life, or both
  • • Your trail group and pace track (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
  • • The rhythm for the weekend so you know exactly what’s coming and how to show up well

The speakers don’t disappear after they talk. You’ll spend real time with them on the trails the next day.

D2

DAY TWO: CONNECTION & MOMENTUM

SATURDAY: 08:00 - 21:00

Saturday is where the Summit becomes different. The morning happens on the trails: guided hikes from Sun Valley Lodge with beginner, intermediate, and advanced options. Each group is led by a keynote speaker, so the conversations continue outside the room, where people are more present and less performative.

In the afternoon we bring it back inside and turn what surfaced into something practical: priorities, systems, and a plan you can execute when you get home.

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • Time with a keynote speaker in a small group setting, not a quick hello after a session
  • • Real connection with peers you’ll actually want to stay in touch with
  • • A clearer sense of what’s been stalling you and what needs to change
  • • A draft 90-day Summit plan: priorities, next steps, and simple metrics to stay honest

The goal isn’t to impress anyone. It’s to leave with momentum you can measure.

D3

DAY THREE: COMMITMENT & THE RETURN

SUNDAY: 08:00 - 16:00

Sunday is a full day of integration, running until 4:00 PM. We begin with an optional early morning recovery session, followed by a final series of workshops designed to lock in your commitments.

After a group lunch, we move into the closing ceremonies and 'Rope Team' formation. This isn't a rush to the airport; it's a dedicated time to ensure you leave with a fully formed 90-day execution plan and the accountability structures to support it.

The Summit officially concludes at 4:00 PM, giving you ample time to reflect before returning to the "real world."

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • Your final 90-day plan, tightened and committed
  • • Your rope team: the people you’ll stay accountable to after Sun Valley
  • • A clear set of non-negotiables around focus, energy, and follow-through
  • • A sense of direction that holds when the noise comes back

The weekend ends, but the point is what happens next.

ESSENTIAL GEAR

  • 01. BROKEN-IN BOOTS Do not bring new boots. Your feet are your engine. Protect them.
  • 02. HARD SHELL LAYERS Weather turns fast at 9,000ft. Waterproof shell + puffer. No cotton.
  • 03. HYDRATION (2L+) CamelBak or Nalgenes. High altitude dehydrates you instantly.
  • 04. OFFLINE ESSENTIALS Notebook and pen. Digital noise is the enemy of clarity.

FIELD NOTES (FAQ)

HOW FIT DO I NEED TO BE?

Comfortable walking 3-5 miles at altitude. If you can walk 18 holes of golf, you can do this.

WILL I HAVE CELL SERVICE?

Yes, but we encourage "Airplane Mode". Look up, not down.

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

"Mountain Ready." Function over fashion. Leave the blazer at home.

The arena is outside. Boardrooms don't build character; mountains do.
