A WARM WELCOME FROM MARK AND THE TEAM

I've climbed the Seven Summits. Played in the NFL. Built companies from scratch.

And I can tell you this: the mountains we choose to climb matter more than the ones life throws at us.

This September in Sun Valley, I'm bringing together 150 leaders for something different. No conference rooms. No PowerPoints. Just three days of intensive leadership training combined with real physical challenges that will push you beyond what you think you're capable of.

I've handpicked Tom Flick and Mariel Hemingway to join me. Together, we'll guide you through the exact frameworks I've used to summit the world's highest peaks and build successful ventures.

Here's the truth: You didn't get where you are by playing it safe. But somewhere along the way, many of us stop climbing. We plateau. We optimize. We manage.

This retreat is your chance to break that pattern. To remember what it feels like to have a goal that scares you.

The spots are limited. We're not filling a stadium we're building a tribe.

If something inside you is stirring right now, that's not anxiety. That's the call.

This fall, we rise.

Mark Pattison

Former NFL Player | Seven Summits Climber
