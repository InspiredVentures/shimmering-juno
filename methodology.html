THE FRAMEWORK

HOW WE CLIMB DICTATES WHERE WE END UP.

Mark Pattison’s success—from the NFL to the Seven Summits—wasn’t built on talent alone. It was built on a repeatable system. When you strip away the pads and the climbing gear, the steps to conquering any "Everest" remain the same.

S

SEED

THE VISION

Everything begins with a single thought. A seed of possibility. You must first dare to plant the idea.

U

UNDERSTANDING

THE PREPARATION

A dream without a plan is just a wish. Research, logistics, and honest assessment of what resources you need.

M

MINDSET

THE INNER GAME

We work on resilience tools—visualization, positive self-talk, and "embracing the suck"—to ensure your mental state is your asset.

M

MOMENTUM

THE ACTION

Consistency over intensity. Small, daily actions that compound over time to create an unstoppable force.

I

IDENTIFY

THE OBSTACLES

Anticipate roadblocks and identify limiting beliefs that arise when the going gets tough.

T

TRAVERSE

THE PIVOT

Sometimes you must move sideways to move up. It’s finding a new way to the same goal.

S

SUMMIT

THE EXECUTION

The final push. It's about how you use that view to find your next Everest.
