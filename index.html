What is
the 7 Summits Summit

You can learn a lot from a book or a podcast. But the hard part isn’t information, it’s the follow-through. When life is noisy, your calendar is full, and the people around you don’t really get what you’re trying to build.

7 Summits Summit is a premium weekend at Sun Valley Lodge built around Mark Pattison’s SUMMITS methodology. You’ll use the same framework Mark teaches for finding direction, building discipline, and staying the course when life gets busy and the plan gets tested.

This is not a sit down, get inspired, go home programme.

The difference is simple: the speakers join you on the trails. Each day includes guided hikes from Sun Valley Lodge with beginner, intermediate, and advanced options. You’ll hike with a speaker and a small group, so the conversations continue where they actually stick: outside, moving, and away from the usual distractions.

You’ll leave with:

Your Everest in focus

You’ll define the summit that matters most right now and the WHY behind it, not a vague list of goals.

Your "Pack Your Bag" Foundation

Fuel, body, mindset, focus, gratitude, and removing the negative voices that quietly sabotage momentum.

A rope team you can trust

You’ll build your support team and leave with real peers to stay accountable, not just new contacts.

A SUMMITS plan you can execute

A written plan with milestones, actions, and simple metrics so you can measure progress and adjust without losing direction.

The ability to TRAVERSE when life hits

Because it will. You’ll leave knowing how to zigzag around setbacks without taking shortcuts or quitting.

Unstoppable Momentum

Practical habits to keep climbing long after the weekend ends. You won't just leave with a plan, you'll leave with velocity.

And yes, we keep it real: this weekend asks for commitment. Mark’s line is clear, it takes a little more to be a champion, and the work begins with what you do next.

So what’s your Everest and what happens when you commit to climbing it?

Your Host

After a career in the NFL, Mark Pattison set a new goal: to climb the Seven Summits. He became the first NFL player to scale the highest peak on every continent.

Now, he translates the discipline, grit, and team dynamics of elite sports and mountaineering into a framework for business leaders and entrepreneurs.

The Lineup

With you every step of the way will be speakers at the top of their game. Each have been on their own epic journey and ready to share the secrets to their success.

These are not motivational speakers.

They are people who have tested themselves in high-stakes environments and come back with clarity. They speak from experience, not theory, and focus on what actually works when things get hard.

Every session is designed to leave you stronger, clearer, and more capable.

The Setup (Agenda)

D1

DAY ONE: CONTEXT & FOUNDATION

FRIDAY NIGHT

You’ll hear from a small group of keynote speakers who’ve built real things and navigated real transitions. Honest stories about what worked, what didn’t, and what they learned the hard way.

Then we set the weekend up properly: how the SUMMITS methodology works, how your trail groups will run, and how to use these three days to get clear on what matters next.

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • A clear understanding of the SUMMITS roadmap and how we’ll apply it across the weekend
  • • Your first draft of an Everest worth pursuing now, in business, life, or both
  • • Your trail group and pace track (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
  • • The rhythm for the weekend so you know exactly what’s coming and how to show up well

The speakers don’t disappear after they talk. You’ll spend real time with them on the trails the next day.

D2

DAY TWO: CONNECTION & MOMENTUM

SATURDAY

Saturday is where the Summit becomes different. The morning happens on the trails: guided hikes from Sun Valley Lodge with beginner, intermediate, and advanced options. Each group is led by a keynote speaker, so the conversations continue outside the room, where people are more present and less performative.

In the afternoon we bring it back inside and turn what surfaced into something practical: priorities, systems, and a plan you can execute when you get home.

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • Time with a keynote speaker in a small group setting, not a quick hello after a session
  • • Real connection with peers you’ll actually want to stay in touch with
  • • A clearer sense of what’s been stalling you and what needs to change
  • • A draft 90-day Summit plan: priorities, next steps, and simple metrics to stay honest

The goal isn’t to impress anyone. It’s to leave with momentum you can measure.

D3

DAY THREE: COMMITMENT & THE RETURN

SUNDAY

Sunday is about integration. We begin with an optional early reset, then we bring the group together to lock in your commitments and make the plan real.

This is where you choose what you’re taking back into everyday life: what you’ll protect, what you’ll stop, and what you’ll do next. We close by building your rope team for the months ahead, so you leave with support, not just inspiration.

YOU’LL WALK AWAY WITH:

  • • Your final 90-day plan, tightened and committed
  • • Your rope team: the people you’ll stay accountable to after Sun Valley
  • • A clear set of non-negotiables around focus, energy, and follow-through
  • • A sense of direction that holds when the noise comes back

The weekend ends, but the point is what happens next.
