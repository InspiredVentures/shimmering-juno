The Challenge

Your Everest is an active challenge. Something you choose to pursue. Something that requires conscious effort, focus, and unwavering determination.

Pursuing it identifies your purpose and expands your capacity to handle everything else life throws at you.

The Game

You may have won the financial game, but what about the game of life?

Fulfillment and purpose doesn't come from comfort. It comes from the "game"—the pursuit of a worthy challenge that demands your full attention. Without a summit, you're just wandering aimlessly through life, lacking purpose.

The Experience

Experience leads to gain: clarity and focus on your goal.

We provide the framework. The lineup—who join you in the mountains of Sun Valley—provides the inspiration and the rope team to support you. You bring an open mind and the desire to succeed.

The Result

Making the Impossible, Possible.

This is not a sit down, get inspired, go home programme.

The difference is simple: the speakers join you on the trails. Each day includes guided hikes from Sun Valley Lodge with beginner, intermediate, and advanced options. You’ll hike with a speaker and a small group, so the conversations continue where they actually stick: outside, moving, and away from the usual distractions.

You’ll leave with:

Your Everest in focus

You’ll define the summit that matters most right now and the WHY behind it, not a vague list of goals.

Your "Pack Your Bag" Foundation

Fuel, body, mindset, focus, gratitude, and removing the negative voices that quietly sabotage momentum.

A rope team you can trust

You’ll build your support team and leave with real peers to stay accountable, not just new contacts.

A SUMMITS plan you can execute

A written plan with milestones, actions, and simple metrics so you can measure progress and adjust without losing direction.

Your Host

After a career in the NFL, Mark Pattison set a new goal: to climb the Seven Summits. He became the first NFL player to scale the highest peak on every continent.

Now, he translates the discipline, grit, and team dynamics of elite sports and mountaineering into a framework for business leaders and entrepreneurs.

The Lineup

All all-star line up of America's most inspirational rockstars!

With you every step of the way will be speakers at the top of their game. Each have been on their own epic journey and ready to share the secrets to their success.

These are not motivational speakers.

They are people who have tested themselves in high-stakes environments and come back with clarity. They speak from experience, not theory, and focus on what actually works when things get hard.
